Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in to Deoit using Google or GitHub authentication, we receive:

  • Your display name and email address (from your Google or GitHub account)
  • Your profile picture (if available)
  • A unique user ID assigned by the authentication provider

We do not collect any additional personal information beyond what is provided by your chosen authentication provider.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate you and provide access to the Service
  • To display your name and profile picture within the application
  • To improve the Service based on usage patterns
  • To communicate with you about service updates or issues (only if you contact us first)

3. Data Storage

Your code projects are stored locally in your browser's localStorage. When you sign in, we may associate your projects with your account for cloud backup purposes. We do not read, analyze, or share your code with any third party.

4. Third-Party Services

Deoit uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication services. Firebase may collect basic device and usage information as described in Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts: Font rendering. Google Fonts may log requests as described in Google's Fonts FAQ.

5. Cookies

Deoit uses essential cookies for authentication purposes via Firebase. We do not use tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or advertising cookies. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit (HTTPS) and secure authentication protocols. However, no online service is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Export your project data at any time using the Export feature

To exercise these rights, contact us at deoit.platform@gmail.com.

8. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal information within 30 days. Your locally stored projects can be deleted at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage.

9. Children's Privacy

Deoit is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, we will delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated revision date.

11. Contact
